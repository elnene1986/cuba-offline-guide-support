# Cuba Offline Guide — Support

The offline travel guide for Cuba: 197 curated places, 21 offline city maps, live exchange rates, emergency info and Cuban phrases — all working with no signal.

## FAQ

**Does the app need internet?**
No. Maps, places, phrases and emergency info are fully offline. Exchange rates update when you're online and the last known rate is kept offline.

**How do I restore my Cuba Pro purchase?**
Money tab → Restore Purchases. Purchases are tied to your Apple ID.

**A place is wrong or closed?**
Email us or use "Help us improve" in the app — we verify and update every report.

## Privacy

The app collects no personal data and requires no account.
